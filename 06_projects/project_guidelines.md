# 🏗 Project Guidelines

Follow this structure for every project:

---

## 1. Problem

What are you solving?

## 2. Data

Where does data come from?

## 3. Preprocessing

- Cleaning
- Scaling
- Feature engineering

## 4. Model

- Which model?
- Why?

## 5. Evaluation

- Metrics used

## 6. Conclusion

- What did you learn?

---

## 🔥 Pro Tip

> "Projects show your thinking, not just your code."
